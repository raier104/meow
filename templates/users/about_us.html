About Our Service

MEOW connects pet owners with verified veterinary clinics across Bangladesh. Users can view doctor profiles, check availability, and book appointments online or offline. Our platform also supports live video consultations, making veterinary care more accessible than ever.

Through our dedicated e-commerce section, users can find high-quality pet food, medicines, litter, and accessories. Sellers can list their products securely, and buyers can shop with confidence using integrated payment gateways like bKash, Nagad, and cards.

MEOW ensures smooth interaction between pet owners, clinics, and sellers. Users receive real-time notifications, upload prescriptions, and rate services. With admin monitoring and multilingual support, the platform fosters a trustworthy and user-friendly environment for all pet care needs.
